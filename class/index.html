The Class

In Kent State University's first-ever Responsive Web Design course, you will learn the fundamental principles for developing standards-based web sites from a mobile-first and content-first perspective and ensure its functionality for users in terms of both usability and performance.

Getting to IdeaBase

IdeaBase is a collaborative, student-powered agency that’s run through the College of Communication & Information in Downtown Kent. IdeaBase is located in Acorn Alley on the second floor above 4 Cats Art Studio and Flashers Cleaners. Visit the IdeaBase website to get more detailed directions.

You can of course walk or bike to class along the esplanade, but if you’re driving, you can park for FREE in any of the public or metered spots on Main Street after 5:00 p.m. Plus, you have access to all of the amenities that downtown Kent has to offer before and after class, including Tree City Coffee (my favorite).
